    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is MyFanSpace?
    MyFanSpace is a next-generation creator platform that combines AI-driven personalization, AR/3D avatars, and Web3 features. It allows creators to build, engage, and monetize their fanbase with advanced tools.
    How is MyFanSpace unique?
    Unlike other platforms, MyFanSpace offers AI-powered smart recommendations, 3D avatar interactions, and flexible monetization tools. We prioritize creator-friendly revenue models with 85% earnings going directly to creators.
    What are the content policies for NSFW material?
    MyFanSpace has a dedicated, age-verified NSFW section with strict privacy controls. All NSFW content must follow legal regulations, require age verification, and comply with community guidelines.
    What are the subscription plans and pricing?
    We offer: Basic (Free)- Access to standard features. Premium ($29/month) - Unlocks advanced analytics, better visibility, and engagement tools. Prime Creator ($79/month) - Full platform access, higher earnings, and priority support. Creators set their own subscription prices and keep 85% of revenue.
    What payment methods are accepted?
    We support credit/debit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrencies. Creators can choose bank transfers, PayPal, or crypto payouts, secured with fraud protection & encryption.
    How does the verification process work?
    Verification requires a government-issued ID, facial recognition, and proof of content ownership. Verified creators get a blue checkmark and higher payout limits.
    Can I migrate my content from other platforms?
    Yes! MyFanSpace provides automated migration tools to import content, subscriber lists, and pricing settings from other platforms. VIP creators get personalized migration assistance.
    When is the global launch?
    MyFanSpace rolls out in phases: March 2025 - Early access for verified creators. Q2 2025 - Beta launch in select regions. Q4 2025 - Full global availability. Sign up early to get exclusive launch benefits!
    How does MyFanSpace handle copyright and content protection?
    MyFanSpace has advanced anti-piracy protection to safeguard creator content. We use: Automated content fingerprinting to track and remove unauthorized reuploads. DMCA takedown tools for quick removal of stolen content. Blockchain verification (optional) for proof of content ownership. Creators own 100% of their content and have full control over licensing and distribution.
